I am a first-year PhD candidate at HKUST NLP Group. My PhD supervisor is Professor Junxian He.

I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2024. My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning.

## Research Interests

- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM truthfulness and Interpretability
